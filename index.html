The Baltimore Cyber Games

What is The Cyber Games?
The Cyber Games is an annual competition for Baltimore County public middle school students hosted at Dumbarton Middle School. At the Cyber Games, students learn cybersecurity and technology skills while competing in a fun, beginner-friendly event with friends. They also have the opportunity to hear from technology professionals, meet high school technology clubs, and win prizes donated by our sponsors.

Who organizes The Cyber Games?
The event is organized by high school students from Eastern Technical High School, Towson High School, and the George Washington Carver Center for Arts and Technology, alongside a teacher advisor from Dumbarton Middle School.

Why compete?
Competing lets you explore the exciting world of technology and cybersecurity, meet professionals in the field, gain valuable real-life skills in a fun way, and win awesome prizes — all while working with your friends!
